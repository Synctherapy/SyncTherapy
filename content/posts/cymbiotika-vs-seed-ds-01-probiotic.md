                            <i class="fas fa-chevron-down group-open:rotate-180 transition-transform"></i>
                        </summary>
                        <p class="mt-4 text-gray-700">A synbiotic is a supplement that contains both probiotics (good bacteria) and prebiotics (food for the good bacteria). This combination is beneficial because the prebiotics help the probiotics to survive, thrive, and colonize in your gut, making the supplement more effective. Seed DS-01 is a synbiotic.</p>
                    </details>
                    <details class="bg-white p-6 rounded-lg shadow-sm group">
                        <summary class="font-semibold text-lg cursor-pointer flex justify-between items-center text-primary">

                            <i class="fas fa-chevron-down group-open:rotate-180 transition-transform"></i>
                        </summary>
                        <p class="mt-4 text-gray-700">Your stomach is highly acidic and can destroy many probiotic bacteria before they ever reach your intestines. An advanced delivery system, like Seed's ViaCap®, protects the live organisms from stomach acid, ensuring they are released in the colon where they can provide the most benefit. This dramatically increases the supplement's effectiveness.</p>

                        </summary>

It's considered a more accurate measure of a probiotic's potency, and it's the standard Seed uses.</p>
