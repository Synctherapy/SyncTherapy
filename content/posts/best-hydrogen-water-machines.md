<h2 class="text-3xl font-bold mb-4">What Is a Hydrogen Water Machine?</h2>
<div class="bg-white p-8 rounded-lg shadow-md space-y-6">
<p>A hydrogen water machine is a device that infuses regular drinking water with molecular hydrogen (H₂) gas through electrolysis. The result is hydrogen-rich water containing dissolved hydrogen molecules—the smallest and most bioavailable antioxidant known to science.</p>

<p>Unlike regular water (H₂O), which has hydrogen atoms bound to oxygen, hydrogen water contains <em>free</em> molecular hydrogen gas your body can actually use. Think of it like the difference between oxygen in water versus oxygen you breathe—same element, very different bioavailability.</p>

<div class="bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-primary p-6 rounded-lg">
    <h4 class="text-xl font-semibold text-primary mb-2">Key Specification: PPM (Parts Per Million)</h4>
    <p>PPM measures dissolved hydrogen concentration. Research studies typically use <strong>0.5-1.6 PPM</strong> for therapeutic effects. Quality machines deliver 1.0+ PPM consistently—many cheap machines fail to reach even 0.5 PPM despite marketing claims. The Echo Flask achieves up to <strong>8 PPM</strong>, far exceeding the therapeutic threshold.</p>
</div>
