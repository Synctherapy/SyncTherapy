            <h2><i class="fas fa-question-circle text-accent mr-2"></i>What Is a Hydrogen Water Machine?</h2>
            <div class="bg-white p-6 rounded-lg shadow-md">
                <p class="mb-4">A <strong>hydrogen water machine</strong> is a device that infuses regular drinking water with molecular hydrogen (H₂) gas through electrolysis. The result is hydrogen-rich water containing dissolved hydrogen molecules—the smallest and most bioavailable antioxidant known to science.</p>
                
                <p class="mb-4">Unlike regular water (H₂O), which has hydrogen atoms bound to oxygen, hydrogen water contains <em>free</em> molecular hydrogen gas your body can actually use. Think of it like the difference between oxygen in water versus oxygen you breathe—same element, very different bioavailability.</p>

                <div class="bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-primary p-4 rounded mb-6">
                    <h3 class="text-xl font-bold mb-2">Key Specification: PPM (Parts Per Million)</h3>
                    <p>PPM measures dissolved hydrogen concentration. Research studies typically use <strong>0.5-1.6 PPM</strong> for therapeutic effects. Quality machines deliver 1.0+ PPM consistently—many cheap machines fail to reach even 0.5 PPM despite marketing claims. The Echo Flask achieves up to <strong>8 PPM</strong>, far exceeding the therapeutic threshold.</p>
                </div>
