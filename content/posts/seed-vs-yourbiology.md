        <section id="key-differences" class="scroll-mt-24 space-y-8">
            <h2 class="text-3xl font-extrabold text-gray-900 border-b pb-4">Key Differences That Matter</h2>

            <div class="prose max-w-none text-gray-700 text-lg leading-relaxed">
                <h3 class="text-2xl font-bold text-gray-900 mt-8 mb-4">Strain Diversity: 24 vs 10</h3>
                <p>Seed's 24-strain formula provides significantly broader bacterial coverage compared to YourBiology's 10 strains. More strains means more diverse microbiome support, which correlates with better overall gut health outcomes. In Seed's 2026 clinical trial, the multi-strain formula increased both Bifidobacterium and Lactobacillus populations significantly.</p>

                <h3 class="text-2xl font-bold text-gray-900 mt-8 mb-4">Potency: AFU vs CFU</h3>
                <p>Seed delivers 53.6 billion AFU (Active Fermentation Units) while YourBiology provides 20 billion CFU (Colony Forming Units). The AFU metric is more comprehensive as it measures viable cells capable of fermentation and reproduction, not just live cells at time of manufacture. Seed's ViaCap technology also ensures the organisms survive stomach acid — the company claims 100% delivery to the intestines.</p>

                <h3 class="text-2xl font-bold text-gray-900 mt-8 mb-4">Delivery Technology: ViaCap vs MAKtrek</h3>
                <p>Both products use advanced delivery technology to protect bacteria from stomach acid. Seed's ViaCap uses a capsule-in-capsule design with the prebiotic in the outer layer and probiotic in the inner capsule. YourBiology's MAKtrek is described as a bypass technology specifically designed to deliver organisms to the gut. Both eliminate the need for refrigeration.</p>

                <h3 class="text-2xl font-bold text-gray-900 mt-8 mb-4">Prebiotic Approach</h3>
                <p>Seed uses pomegranate punicalagins and pine bark polyphenols as prebiotics — these are non-fermenting, meaning they won't cause bloating like traditional prebiotics (inulin/FOS). YourBiology uses inulin and FOS, which are more fermentable and may cause gas in sensitive individuals. However, YourBiology includes digestive enzymes and Vitamin D3 as additional benefits.</p>

                <h3 class="text-2xl font-bold text-gray-900 mt-8 mb-4">Clinical Evidence</h3>
                <p>Seed has significant clinical backing with multiple 2024-2026 randomized controlled trials published in peer-reviewed journals like Nutrients. YourBiology relies more on technology claims rather than published human clinical trials on their specific formula. For evidence-based consumers, Seed is the clear winner.</p>
            </div>
        </section>
