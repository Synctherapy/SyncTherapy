## Frequently Asked Questions
What is the Viome Gut Intelligence Test?
\\\\_\\\\_\\\\_PRESERVED\\\\_JSX\\\\_BLOCK\\\\_17\\\\_\\\\_\\\\_
The [Viome Gut Intelligence Test](/viome-review/) is a comprehensive at-home test that analyzes your gut microbiome using advanced RNA sequencing to provide personalized health insights and diet recommendations. With its detailed analysis, the Viome Gut Intelligence Test not only educates you about your gut health but also empowers you to make informed dietary choices. As part of the growing trend in personalized health, it stands out among the [best gut health testing options](/gut-health-test/) available today. By understanding the unique composition of your gut microbiome, you can optimize your well-being and enhance your overall health.
Does Viome test for parasites?
\\\\_\\\\_\\\\_PRESERVED\\\\_JSX\\\\_BLOCK\\\\_18\\\\_\\\\_\\\\_
While Viome's gut tests are comprehensive in analyzing bacteria, viruses, fungi, and archaea, they do not specifically test for or identify parasites. It is not a diagnostic test for parasitic infections.
How accurate and reliable are Viome's results?
\\\\_\\\\_\\\\_PRESERVED\\\\_JSX\\\\_BLOCK\\\\_19\\\\_\\\\_\\\\_
Viome uses state-of-the-art metatranscriptomic sequencing in a CLIA-certified lab, which is considered highly accurate for identifying and quantifying the active microorganisms in your gut. The recommendations are based on a sophisticated AI engine that analyzes this data against a vast scientific database.
