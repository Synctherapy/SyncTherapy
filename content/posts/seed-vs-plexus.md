                        <div>
                            <h3 class="text-2xl font-bold">The MLM Option</h3>
                            <p class="text-accent font-semibold mb-4">Probiotic + Enzyme Combination</p>
                            <p class="mb-4">Plexus ProBio5 is a multi-level marketing product that combines five probiotic strains with four digestive enzymes (protease, amylase, lipase, and lactase). The addition of enzymes is a legitimate differentiator — they may help break down proteins, carbohydrates, fats, and dairy for some users with digestive enzyme deficiencies.</p>
                            <p class="mb-4">Plexus sells through independent consultants, which means you won't find their products in retail stores. The pricing is attractive at approximately $35/month, making it more accessible than Seed's $49.99/month price point.</p>
                            <p class="mb-4">However, from a transparency perspective, there are concerns. Unlike Seed, Plexus does not publish the same level of strain-specific clinical research on their ProBio5 formula. The strain count (5) is considerably lower than Seed's 24 strains, and the potency is not disclosed in the same AFU format that allows for independent verification.</p>
