## Frequently Asked Questions
How long does it take to see results from a RLT panel?
\\\\_\\\\_\\\\_PRESERVED\\\\_JSX\\\\_BLOCK\\\\_16\\\\_\\\\_\\\\_
It's a cumulative process. While some benefits like reduced inflammation can be felt quickly, visible changes in skin or significant pain reduction can take several weeks to months of consistent use. [How often you use red light therapy](/at-home-red-light-therapy/) is key; a frequency of 3-5 times per week for 10-20 minutes is typically recommended based on clinical research.
Is red light therapy safe for your eyes?
\\\\_\\\\_\\\\_PRESERVED\\\\_JSX\\\\_BLOCK\\\\_17\\\\_\\\\_\\\\_
Yes, red and NIR light are generally safe. However, the LEDs are extremely bright. It's always recommended to use the provided protective goggles, especially when the device is aimed at your face or if you're sensitive to bright light. Some research even explores the benefits of [red light therapy for eye health](/red-light-therapy-and-eye-health/), but this should only be done under specific protocols.
What is pulsing and why does it matter?
\\\\_\\\\_\\\\_PRESERVED\\\\_JSX\\\\_BLOCK\\\\_18\\\\_\\\\_\\\\_
Pulsing delivers light in rapid on/off cycles. Some research suggests that pulsing at specific frequencies can enhance cellular response and provide different therapeutic benefits compared to continuous wave light. The G4's wide pulsing range (1-10,000Hz) allows for experimentation with different protocols.
