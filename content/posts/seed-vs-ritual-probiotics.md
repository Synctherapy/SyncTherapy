## What is a Synbiotic?
A synbiotic is a type of [probiotic supplement](/best-prebiotics-and-probiotics-supplements/) that strategically combines \\\*\\\*probiotics\\\*\\\* (beneficial live bacteria) and \\\*\\\*prebiotics\\\*\\\* (specialized fibers that feed those bacteria). The goal is to work synergistically: the prebiotics provide the necessary fuel for the probiotics to thrive and colonize effectively within your gut.
Some modern formulas, like Ritual's, also include \\\*\\\*postbiotics\\\*\\\*. These are the beneficial compounds produced by probiotics when they consume prebiotics. Including a postbiotic provides direct benefits to the gut lining, essentially skipping a step in the metabolic process.
