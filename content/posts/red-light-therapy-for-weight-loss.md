<section id="adipocytes">
    <h2 class="text-3xl font-bold mb-6">How Red Light Therapy Affects Adipocytes (Fat Cells)</h2>
    <p class="mb-4 text-gray-700">Your body stores fat in cells called adipocytes. When you use specific wavelengths of light—usually 635nm to 660nm—a fascinating biological process occurs:</p>
    
    <div class="grid md:grid-cols-3 gap-6 my-8">
        <div class="bg-white p-6 rounded-lg shadow-sm border-t-4 border-accent text-center">
            <div class="bg-primary text-white rounded-full w-12 h-12 flex items-center justify-center font-bold mx-auto mb-4 text-xl">1</div>
            <h3 class="font-bold text-gray-800 mb-2">The Pores Open</h3>
            <p class="text-gray-600 text-sm">Clinical studies have shown that exposing fat cells to red light creates temporary, microscopic pores in the cell membrane.</p>
        </div>
        <div class="bg-white p-6 rounded-lg shadow-sm border-t-4 border-accent text-center">
            <div class="bg-primary text-white rounded-full w-12 h-12 flex items-center justify-center font-bold mx-auto mb-4 text-xl">2</div>
            <h3 class="font-bold text-gray-800 mb-2">Lipolysis Occurs</h3>
            <p class="text-gray-600 text-sm">Once these pores open, the cell releases its stored contents: water, glycerol, and free fatty acids (FFAs) into the interstitial space and lymphatic system.</p>
        </div>
        <div class="bg-white p-6 rounded-lg shadow-sm border-t-4 border-accent text-center">
            <div class="bg-primary text-white rounded-full w-12 h-12 flex items-center justify-center font-bold mx-auto mb-4 text-xl">3</div>
            <h3 class="font-bold text-gray-800 mb-2">The Cells Shrink</h3>
            <p class="text-gray-600 text-sm">The fat cell does not die or get destroyed; rather, it rapidly deflates like a balloon losing air.</p>
        </div>
    </div>
</section>

<section id="the-catch">
    <h2 class="text-3xl font-bold mb-6">The Catch: You Must Burn the Released Fat</h2>
    <p class="mb-4 text-gray-700">Here is where the "magic pill" marketing completely fails.</p>
    <p class="mb-4 text-gray-700">Once those free fatty acids are released into your lymphatic system, your body has to do <em>something</em> with them. If you just lay on the couch after your red light session, your body will eventually sweep those fatty acids right back into the fat cells in a few hours.</p>
    <div class="bg-blue-50 border-l-4 border-primary p-6 rounded-r-lg shadow-sm my-6">
        <p class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-800">To actually achieve weight loss, you must undergo oxidative metabolism.</p>
        <p class="text-gray-700 mt-2">In simple terms: you have to exercise or create a caloric deficit to burn that mobilized fat as energy.</p>
    </div>
</section>
