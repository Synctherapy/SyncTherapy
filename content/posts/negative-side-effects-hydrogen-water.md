## 1. Why Molecular Hydrogen Itself Has No Side Effects

To understand why hydrogen gas (H₂) is so safe, you have to understand its chemical behavior in the human body.

### It Is a "Selective" Antioxidant
Unlike high-dose Vitamin C or Vitamin E supplements, which can act as non-selective antioxidants (meaning they neutralize *all* oxidation, even the good biological signaling your body needs to trigger muscle growth), molecular hydrogen is **highly selective**. 

H₂ exclusively targets and neutralizes only the most cytotoxic (cell-damaging) free radicals, specifically the **hydroxyl radical (•OH)**. It leaves the beneficial free radicals (like nitric oxide, which you need for blood flow) completely alone. 

### It Leaves No Toxic Byproducts
When molecular hydrogen (H₂) neutralizes a toxic hydroxyl radical (•OH), the chemical byproduct of that reaction is simply H₂O (water). Your body just hydrates itself. There is no toxic waste for your liver or kidneys to filter out.

### You Naturally Ingest It Every Day
Your gut microbiome naturally produces up to 10 liters of hydrogen gas every day as a byproduct of fermenting dietary fiber. Your body's evolutionary biology is completely adapted to processing and utilizing molecular hydrogen. You literally cannot "overdose" on it; any excess hydrogen gas your body doesn't need is safely exhaled through your lungs.
