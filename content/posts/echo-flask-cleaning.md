faqs:
  - question: How often should you clean the Echo Flask?
    answer: "Rinse the Echo Flask with clean water daily after use. Wash with mild soap and filtered water weekly. Perform a deep vinegar descaling monthly using the built-in Clean Flask cycle."
  - question: Can you put the Echo Flask in the dishwasher?
    answer: "No. The Echo Flask is hand wash only. Never place it in a dishwasher or submerge it in water. This can damage the electronics and voids the warranty."
  - question: What cleaning solution should I use for the Echo Flask?
    answer: "For monthly deep cleaning, use 1 part white vinegar (any 5% distilled white vinegar) to 2 parts warm distilled water. Never use apple cider vinegar, bleach, or harsh chemicals as these can damage the electrodes and membrane."
  - question: How do you clean the Echo Flask gasket?
    answer: "Use tweezers or a dull object to carefully remove the gray gasket from the lid. Cleanse with mild detergent and filtered water, or soak in the vinegar solution for up to 2 hours. Make sure the gasket and surrounding areas are clean before reinserting with the line side facing inward."
