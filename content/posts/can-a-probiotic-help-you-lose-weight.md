## \\\*\\\*Key TakeAway\\\*\\\* Can a Probiotic Help You Lose Weight?
Probiotics can help with weight loss and belly fat by altering the balance of gut bacteria. Probiotics have been studied for their potential role in weight loss and management.
Research suggests that certain strains of probiotics, such as Lactobacillus gasseri, Lactobacillus rhamnosus, and Bifidobacterium, may help with weight loss by altering the gut microbiome and influencing energy metabolism, appetite regulation, and inflammation.
For instance, a [study](https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/british-journal-of-nutrition/article/effect-of-lactobacillus-gasseri-sbt2055-in-fermented-milk-on-abdominal-adiposity-in-adults-in-a-randomised-controlled-trial/304E3E2EE11E0D3D4F5D85E7046118A1) found that participants who consumed [fermented milk](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/18801055/) products containing Lactobacillus gasseri for 12 weeks experienced a significant reduction in visceral fat and body weight.
Another study showed that Lactobacillus rhamnosus supplementation led to weight loss and improved body composition in overweight individuals.
Probiotics have been found to reduce inflammation, which is associated with obesity, and improve glucose and lipid metabolism.
While the evidence is promising, it is essential to note that probiotics should be used in conjunction with a healthy diet and regular exercise for optimal results.
