            <h2 class="text-2xl font-black text-gray-900 mb-4">LumeBox Overview</h2>
            <p class="text-gray-700">LumeBox 2.0 is a targeted handheld device with 15 × 3W LEDs emitting 660nm red and 850nm NIR. Its key spec is extremely high irradiance at close range — 125 mW/cm² (red) and 140 mW/cm² (NIR) measured at 1 cm. It's designed for short, intense spot treatments rather than broad coverage.
