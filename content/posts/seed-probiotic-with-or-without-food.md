  - question: Should I take Seed probiotic with food or on an empty stomach?
    answer: "Seed DS-01 is best taken on an empty stomach with water — ideally first thing in the morning before breakfast or before bed. However, thanks to ViaCap technology, the timing is less critical than with standard probiotics. The capsule-in-capsule design protects the 24 probiotic strains from stomach acid, ensuring they reach your intestines alive regardless of when you take them."
  - question: What is the best time of day to take Seed DS-01?
    answer: "The optimal time is first thing in the morning on an empty stomach, about 15-30 minutes before breakfast. This allows the probiotic strains to pass through your stomach quickly and reach your intestines where they can colonize. Taking it before bed is also effective since your digestive system is relatively empty."
  - question: Can I take Seed with coffee or other beverages?
    answer: "Yes, you can take Seed with water, but avoid taking it with hot beverages like coffee or tea. The heat could potentially affect the probiotic viability, though ViaCap provides significant protection. For best results, take Seed with room temperature or cold water."
  - question: Does ViaCap technology really make timing less important?
    answer: "Yes. The patented ViaCap capsule-in-capsule delivery system protects the probiotic bacteria from stomach acid.   - question: Can I take Seed with meals?
    answer: "While taking Seed with food isn't harmful, it's not ideal for maximum absorption. Food in your stomach slows gastric emptying, meaning the probiotics spend longer in the acidic environment. For best results, take Seed on an empty stomach — either 15-30 minutes before a meal or 2-3 hours after a meal."
  - question: Should I take Seed before or after antibiotics?
    answer: "Seed should be taken AFTER completing a course of antibiotics, not during. Antibiotics kill both harmful and beneficial bacteria. Once your antibiotic course is finished, wait 2-3 days before starting Seed to allow the antibiotic to fully clear your system, then begin taking it consistently."
