            <section id="how-probiotics-work">
                <h2 class="text-3xl font-bold">How Probiotics Work for Acne: The Gut-Skin Connection</h2>
                <div class="space-y-6 bg-white p-8 rounded-lg shadow-md">
                    <div>
                        <h3 class="text-xl font-semibold text-primary">They Reduce Systemic Inflammation</h4>
                        <p>An imbalanced gut (dysbiosis) can lead to inflammation throughout your body. Probiotics help restore balance in the gut, which in turn calms the systemic inflammation that can trigger acne breakouts.</p>
                    </div>
                    <div>
                        <h3 class="text-xl font-semibold text-primary">They Strengthen the Gut Barrier</h4>
                        <p>Probiotics help reinforce the intestinal lining, preventing undigested food particles and toxins from "leaking" into the bloodstream—a condition known as leaky gut, which is a known contributor to skin issues.</p>
                    </div>
                    <div>
                        <h3 class="text-xl font-semibold text-primary">The Role of Prebiotics (and Synbiotics)</h4>
