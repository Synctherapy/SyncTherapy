## Understanding PEMF Therapy
PEMF Therapy, also known as Pulsed Electromagnetic Field Therapy, is an innovative approach used for alleviating pain and improving the quality of life for those with arthritis.
### What Is PEMF Therapy?
PEMF Therapy involves the use of electromagnetic fields to promote various healing processes in the body.
It is a non-invasive method that sends magnetic energy into the body, which then stimulates electrical changes around and within the cell.
This activation of cells can lead to improved circulation, reduced inflammation, and enhanced tissue repair, making it particularly beneficial for individuals suffering from arthritis.
### History of PEMF Therapy
The concept of using electromagnetic fields for healing dates back to the early 1900s, but it was in the 1970s when PEMF Therapy began to gain traction.
Initially, it was utilized by veterinarians for treating racehorses' broken legs. Following this, the first FDA-approved PEMF device came out in 1979 for stimulating bone growth.
Since then, PEMF Therapy has been continuously researched and developed, leading to the current use in treating various conditions, including arthritis pain relief.
