            <p class="mb-6 text-gray-700">Understanding <strong>how hydrogen water bottles work</strong> helps you avoid scams and choose a quality device. Here's the science made simple:</p>
            
            <div class="grid md:grid-cols-2 gap-8">
                <div>
                    <h3 class="text-xl font-bold mb-3">The Electrolysis Process</h3>
                    <ol class="space-y-3 list-decimal list-inside text-gray-700">
                        <li><strong>You add water</strong> to the bottle (filtered or purified water works best).</li>
                        <li><strong>Electrodes activate</strong> when you press the button, passing an electrical current through the water.</li>
                        <li><strong>Water molecules split</strong> ‚Äî H‚ÇÇO separates into hydrogen gas (H‚ÇÇ) and oxygen gas (O‚ÇÇ).</li>
                        <li><strong>Hydrogen dissolves</strong> into the water, creating hydrogen-rich water.</li>
                        <li><strong>Waste gases vent</strong> ‚Äî Quality bottles use SPE/PEM technology to separate and release chlorine, ozone, and oxygen.</li>
                    </ol>
                </div>
                <div class="bg-blue-50 p-6 rounded-lg">
                    <h3 class="text-xl font-bold mb-3">What is SPE/PEM Technology?</h3>
                    <p class="text-gray-700 mb-4"><strong>SPE (Solid Polymer Electrolysis)</strong> and <strong>PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane)</strong> technology is the gold standard for hydrogen water bottles. This system:</p>
                    <ul class="space-y-2">
                        <li class="flex items-start"><i class="fas fa-check-circle text-green-500 mt-1 mr-2"></i>Produces pure molecular hydrogen (H‚ÇÇ)</li>
                        <li class="flex items-start"><i class="fas fa-check-circle text-green-500 mt-1 mr-2"></i>Separates harmful byproducts (chlorine, ozone)</li>
                        <li class="flex items-start"><i class="fas fa-check-circle text-green-500 mt-1 mr-2"></i>Vents waste gases safely away from drinking water</li>
                        <li class="flex items-start"><i class="fas fa-check-circle text-green-500 mt-1 mr-2"></i>Uses platinum-coated titanium electrodes for durability</li>
                    </ul>
                    <p class="mt-4 text-sm text-red-600"><strong>Warning:</strong> Cheap bottles without SPE/PEM technology may leave chlorine and ozone in your water, which can be harmful.</p>
