### Setting Up for Your First Sauna Session
![]()
Before diving into the warmth of your first sauna session, it's crucial to set the stage for a smooth and enjoyable experience.
This starts with choosing the right location for your sauna blanket.
A quiet, comfortable space where you can relax uninterrupted is ideal.
Ensure the area is well-ventilated to manage the heat and humidity levels generated during your session.
Preparing yourself is also an essential step. Hydrate well before beginning, as you'll be sweating out a lot of fluids.
Wear light, breathable clothing or opt for a towel wrap to maximize the infrared heat's effects on your skin.
Finally, set your intentions for the session, whether it's to relax, detoxify, or relieve muscle tension, to enhance the mental benefits of your sauna experience.
#### The Assembly: Pre-heat and Get Comfortable
Setting up your sauna blanket is a straightforward process. Start by placing it on a heat-resistant surface to protect your floor or furniture.
A yoga mat or a massage table works perfectly for this purpose. Then, select a temperature that suits your comfort level.
It’s recommended to start at a lower temperature for your first few sessions and gradually increase it as you become more accustomed to the heat.
Pre-heating your blanket for about 10 minutes before you get in ensures a warm and inviting experience right from the start.
Once your sauna blanket is ready, it's time to get comfortable. Ensure you have a glass of water nearby to stay hydrated and a towel to wipe away sweat during your session.
If you're using the sauna blanket for relaxation, consider playing some soft music or an audiobook in the background. Remember, the goal is to create a peaceful and comfortable environment that allows you to relax fully and enjoy the benefits of your sweat sessions.
![]()
### Maintenance and Care Tips
To ensure your HigherDOSE infrared sauna blanket remains in top condition, incorporating towel inserts is a smart move.
These inserts act as a barrier, absorbing sweat and oils which, if left unchecked, could seep into the blanket's fabric, potentially damaging its intricate wiring and heating elements.
For optimal performance, it's recommended to lay a towel insert inside the sauna blanket before each use.
This simple step can significantly prolong the life of your sauna blanket, ensuring it remains a cornerstone of your wellness routine for years to come.
In addition to using a towel insert, dry brushing before stepping into your sauna blanket can enhance both the sauna experience and the blanket’s longevity.
Dry brushing helps to remove dead skin cells, promoting cleaner skin and, by extension, a cleaner blanket.
It's advisable to spend about 10 minutes dry brushing to maximize these benefits.
After each sauna session, let the blanket cool down, then gently wipe it down with a damp cloth to remove any residual sweat or oils, ensuring it's completely dry before storing it away.
