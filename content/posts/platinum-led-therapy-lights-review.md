<!-- How PlatinumLED Therapy Works -->
<section id="how-it-works">
<h2 class="text-3xl font-bold mb-4">How PlatinumLED Therapy Lights Work</h2>
<div class="bg-white p-8 rounded-lg shadow-md space-y-6">
<p class="text-gray-700">PlatinumLED therapy lights are advanced photobiomodulation devices that use specific wavelengths of light—primarily red and near-infrared (NIR)—to stimulate healing at a cellular level. The core principle is that this light energy is absorbed by mitochondria, our cellular powerhouses, which <a href="/red-light-therapy/" class="text-content-link">boosts ATP (energy) production</a>, reduces inflammation, and increases circulation.</p>

<img src="/uploads/2025/07/Platinum-LED-BIOMAX-900-Red-Light-review.jpg" alt="Platinum LED BIOMAX 900 Red Light panel in clinical setting" class="rounded-lg shadow-sm w-full" loading="lazy">

<div>
    <h4 class="text-xl font-semibold text-primary mb-2"><i class="fas fa-bolt text-accent mr-2"></i>Boosting Cellular Energy</h4>
    <p>The primary mechanism of photobiomodulation occurs within the mitochondria. Photons within the red and near-infrared spectrum are absorbed by Cytochrome C Oxidase, helping to dissociate inhibitory nitric oxide and restoring the cell's ability to produce ATP—the universal energy currency of the cell.</p>
</div>

<div>
    <h4 class="text-xl font-semibold text-primary mb-2"><i class="fas fa-tint text-accent mr-2"></i>Improving Blood Flow & Reducing Inflammation</h4>
    <p>The release of nitric oxide acts as a potent vasodilator, widening blood vessels to improve local circulation. This increased blood flow delivers more oxygen and nutrients to tissues while helping to remove waste products, effectively reducing inflammation at the source.</p>
</div>
</div>
</section>
